# Generative AI Leader

The Generative AI Leader certification is designed for business leaders, managers, and non-technical professionals who need to understand the strategic implications, business value, and responsible implementation of Generative AI within an organization using Google Cloud.

## Exam Details
- **Level:** Foundational
- **Length:** 90 minutes
- **Format:** Multiple choice and multiple select
- **Cost:** $99 USD

## Key Topics
- **Gen AI Landscape:** Understanding LLMs, diffusion models, and the evolution of AI.
- **Google Cloud AI Portfolio:** Overview of Gemini, Vertex AI, AI Studio, and NotebookLM.
- **Business Strategy:** Identifying use cases, ROI, and organizational readiness.
- **Responsible AI:** Understanding bias, safety, privacy, and Google’s AI Principles.
- **Implementation:** Understanding the lifecycle of a Gen AI project from ideation to deployment.
