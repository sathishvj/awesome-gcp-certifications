# Associate Google Workspace Administrator

This certification focuses on the day-to-day administration of Google Workspace, covering user management, security settings, and service configuration.

## Exam Details
- **Level:** Associate
- **Length:** 120 minutes
- **Format:** Multiple choice and multiple select
- **Cost:** $125 USD

## Key Topics
- **User & Group Management:** Provisioning, lifecycle management, and organizational units (OUs).
- **Service Configuration:** Managing Gmail, Drive, Meet, and Calendar settings.
- **Security & Compliance:** 2-Step Verification, password policies, and data loss prevention (DLP).
- **Endpoint Management:** Mobile device management (MDM) and security policies.
- **Data Governance:** Using Google Vault for eDiscovery and retention.
